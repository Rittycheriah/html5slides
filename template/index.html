Building Relationships...
And why it matters.

An implicit part of being at NSS is learning about the Developer Community of Nashville.

Why say you?

  • It helps you to build a community around your personal brand.
  • It helps us to give back to the community we are going to join after NSS.
  • It's a lot less scary to interview with people who have had genuine conversations with you before.
  • It helps you grow as a developer to have conversations with people with differing views from your own.

So how can you get involved?


  • Follow the NashDev Community Twitter - @nashdevcal
  • Go to Meetups! - http://www.meetup.com/
  • Go to the Code For Nashville Hack Night to work on a project with other devs.
  • Try out a Code and Pinot Night with the Girl Geek Dinner
  • Go to any and all hackathons.
  • Remember your closest friends through this journey are your classmates.

Interested in other avenues other than Meetups?

  • Conferences
  • NashCocktail
  • User Group Lunches
  • Help with Code Day or other community educational initiatives.

Feeling awkward?

  • Buddy System
  • Ask others about their interests
  • Ask questions about the technology presented
  • Be yourself!
